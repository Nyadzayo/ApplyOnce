## Description
Tired of typing the same 20 answers into every job application? ApplyOnce
fills applications on Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, and thousands of career
sites from a profile stored only on your device — and it never submits
anything for you.

In this video: drop in your resume once, open any job posting, click Fill.
Green means verified, amber means check me, and one-click Undo restores any
form instantly. Your resume file attaches itself, and a built-in tracker
keeps your whole job search organized with statuses and follow-up reminders.

Free and open source. No account, no cloud, no tracking.
